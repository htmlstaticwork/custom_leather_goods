Rooted in Tradition

A decade of perfecting the art of slow-hand craftsmanship.

The Heritage Story

Heritage & Hide began in 2014 in a small garage workshop in Portland, Oregon. What started as a personal quest to create a belt that wouldn't fall apart after a year turned into a lifelong obsession with functional longevity.

I spent thousands of hours learning traditional saddlery techniques - the kind of skills that are slowly being forgotten in the age of automation. Today, every piece that leaves our workshop is still made by those same hands, using the same traditional tools.

2014

Founded Heritage & Hide with just one workbench and a hide of bridle leather.

2016

Launched our Signature Minimalist Collection, gaining recognition for functional simplicity.

2019

Moved to our current workshop and expanded to heritage-grade bags and carry goods.

2024

Celebrating a decade of craftsmanship and over 5,000 heirloom pieces commissioned worldwide.

Our Philosophy

Quality Above All

We source our leather from world-renowned tanneries like Horween and Wickett & Craig. We only use full-grain, vegetable-tanned hides because they are the only material that improves with age.

Functional Longevity

We don't design for trends. We design for the person who wants to buy something once and use it for twenty years. Every design is field-tested for months before release.
